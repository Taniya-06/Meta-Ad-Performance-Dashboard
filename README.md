# Meta-Ad-Performance-Dashboard

This interactive **Meta Ad Performance Dashboard** provides a comprehensive analysis of advertising performance across **Facebook and Instagram campaigns**. The dashboard enables marketing teams to track, compare, and optimize ad performance through visual insights on impressions, engagement, conversions, and audience demographics.

### **Objective**

The goal of this project is to **analyze and compare Meta ad campaign performance** across multiple dimensions—such as demographics, geography, time, and ad type—to identify patterns that drive engagement and conversion. The insights help stakeholders make **data-driven marketing decisions** and improve ad effectiveness.

### **Key Highlights**

* **Total Impressions:** Facebook – *216K*, Instagram – *123.8K*
* **Total Engagements:** Facebook – *29.3K*, Instagram – *16.8K*
* **Clicks:** Facebook – *25.4K*, Instagram – *14.7K*
* **Conversion Rate:** Facebook – *5.21%*, Instagram – *4.82%*
* **Purchase Rate:** Facebook – *0.61%*, Instagram – *0.57%*
* **Total Budget:** *$2.5M* with an **average campaign cost** of *$50.7K*

### **Audience Insights**

* **Gender:** Engagement is higher among **female users** (43% on Facebook and 37% on Instagram).
* **Age Group:** The majority of engagement comes from the **18–30 age segment**, showing that younger audiences are more active and responsive to Meta ads.
* **Geography:** Strong engagement across **North America, Europe, and Asia**, indicating a global audience reach.

### **Performance by Ad Type**

* **Facebook:** Stories ads delivered the **highest impressions (23.8K)** and engagement rate (13.56%).
* **Instagram:** Image ads performed consistently well with strong click-through (11.86%) and engagement rates (13.60%).
* **Video and Carousel ads** showed moderate engagement but higher conversion potential.

### **Trend Analysis**

* **Weekly and Hourly Trends** reveal peak engagement during mid-week and afternoon hours, suggesting optimal times for ad placements.
* **Monthly Analysis (June)** shows consistent engagement, highlighting stable campaign performance.

### **Conclusion**

The **Meta Ad Performance Dashboard** effectively summarizes campaign performance, helping stakeholders **evaluate ROI**, **understand audience behavior**, and **optimize ad strategies**. It demonstrates the power of **data visualization in digital marketing analytics**, offering actionable insights to enhance future campaigns.
